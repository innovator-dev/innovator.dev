Platform for civic involvement and innovation

Business requirements

We live in an increasingly complex reality where societies change rapidly, so are the communities in which we live. To generate the changes we want in our communities requires more civic spirit and trust in individual and collective power.

The platform for civic involvement and innovation is a mechanism to identify, connect and amplify the impact of local civic initiatives that contribute to improve the quality of life and strengthening local democracy.

Technical solution

The goal has been to build a platform which empowers individuals to actively participate in shaping their communities, providing a digital space where ideas flourish and collaboration thrives. After careful consideration, we've chosen to develop a custom web solution, ensuring a tailored and seamless user experience.

A custom approach allowed us to address the unique needs and intricacies of civic involvement and innovation, providing a user-friendly interface that aligns perfectly with the vision of the program.

Feature highlights

  • management system for civic initiatives and users
  • voting mechanism for founders to prioritize initiative funding

Time to market

In the fast-paced landscape of innovation, time is a critical factor in bringing ideas to life, so we've launched the first phase of the platform in just one month, going live on November 17th, 2023.
