Fundraising donation web application for non-profit

Business requirements

For this project, the business requirement has been to build a simple page where visitors could get informed about the situation from Ukraine and Russia, fill in a survey and donate through PayPal for Nonprofits.

Technical solution

For the given business requirement, our approach has been to build an single-page application (SPA) dynamically interacting with visitors without the need to traverse multiple pages.

Feature highlights

  • multi-language platform
  • custom donation mechanism through PayPal for Nonprofits

Time to market

The development of the application took 1 week, project kick-off on April 28th and went live on May 6th, 2022.
