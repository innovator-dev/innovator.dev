Fundraising donation web application for nonprofits

Business requirements

In response to urgent humanitarian needs caused by the war in Ukraine, our client needed a simple but effective digital fundraising experience for an NGO supporting communities in crisis. The goal was to inform visitors about the situation, gather feedback through a short survey, and make online donations fast and accessible from any device.

Traditional donation flows were fragmented and often created friction for supporters. The organization needed a lightweight web application that could combine awareness, engagement, and giving in one place, helping convert visitor attention into measurable support.

Technical solution

We engineered built a lightweight single-page web application focused on speed, clarity, and ease of use. The application brings together informational content, a survey module, and PayPal donation flows in a single responsive experience optimized for desktop and mobile users.

The solution was intentionally compact to reduce friction and accelerate launch. By combining modern frontend implementation with secure payment integration, the platform helped the NGO publish urgent campaign information quickly and support immediate fundraising efforts.

Feature highlights

  • Fast single-page experience with responsive design and smooth navigation, allowing visitors to access information, share feedback, and donate without unnecessary steps
  • Secure PayPal integration for fast online donations, reducing friction in the contribution flow and supporting a trusted payment experience
  • Survey module for supporter feedback to capture opinions and improve understanding of visitor sentiment around the campaign
  • Mobile-friendly design to ensure strong usability across devices during high-urgency awareness and donation campaigns

Time to market

The application launched on May 6, 2022, just one week after the initial requirements were defined. This rapid delivery enabled the organization to respond quickly to a time-sensitive humanitarian situation and begin fundraising without delay.

The result was a focused digital donation experience that simplified giving, improved visitor engagement, and gave the nonprofit a modern tool for fast campaign deployment.
