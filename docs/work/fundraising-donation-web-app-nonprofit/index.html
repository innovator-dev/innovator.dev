Fundraising donation web application for non-profit

Business requirements

As business requirement, we were asked to build a simple page where visitors could get informed about the situation from Ukraine and Russia, share their opinion through a survey and donate for an NGO.

Technical solution

For the given business requirement, our approach has been to build an single-page application (SPA) dynamically interacting with visitors in a simple, but modern manner and enable donation through PayPal for Nonprofits.

Feature highlights

  • multi-language platform
  • custom donation mechanism through PayPal for Nonprofits

Time to market

The development of the application took 1 week, project kick-off on April 28th and went live on May 6th, 2022.
