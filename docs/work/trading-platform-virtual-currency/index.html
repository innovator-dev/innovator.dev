Loyalty-to-accommodation trading platform

Business requirements

The customer wanted to launch a loyalty-to-accommodation trading platform where users can buy and sell a custom virtual currency and use it to secure hotel stays at partner properties. Holding a minimum of 1,000 coins for one year as a deposit grants the right to stay at any enrolled hotel unit, turning loyalty into a flexible accommodation benefit.

They needed a secure, real-time platform that combines trading capabilities (similar to a stock market experience) with clear rules for deposits, balances, and redemption, all wrapped in a user-friendly experience for both mobile and desktop users.

Technical solution

We engineered an API-driven platform designed for low-latency operations and real-time trading. The backend exposes a robust set of APIs for order placement, wallet management, deposits, and settlement, while the frontend delivers a responsive interface optimized for both mobile and desktop trading.

The architecture supports secure wallet operations (top-ups, withdrawals, deposits), multi-language support, and clear transaction flows, ensuring users can confidently manage their virtual coins and understand how they translate into hotel stay rights.

Feature highlights

  • Real-time order execution: Users can place buy and sell orders for the virtual currency with near real-time execution, mirroring familiar trading experiences
  • Multi-language experience: Platform available in English and Romanian, making it accessible to a broader regional user base
  • Wallet and funds management: Users can top up their accounts, withdraw funds, and view balances and transaction history in a dedicated wallet interface
  • Transaction lifecycle management: Create, execute, and cancel trading operations with clear status updates and safeguards to prevent invalid or duplicate transactions
  • Deposit creation and eligibility: Dedicated deposit functionality to lock a minimum number of coins for a year, granting access rights to stays in participating hotel units

Time to market

The platform launched on April 5th, 2022, as a production-ready trading system with real-time order execution and secure transactions. Delivery combined rapid product development with focused performance, load, and security testing to ensure the platform was stable and safe from day one, without compromising on speed of execution.
