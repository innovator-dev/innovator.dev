Trading platform for virtual currency

Business requirements

As high level business requirement, the goal was to build a trading platform where users could purchase or sell a custom virtual currency that aims to solve the essential needs of human beings, food and housing. Owning a minimum of 1000 coins, kept for 1 year as a deposit, would give the owner the right to stay at any of the enrolled partner hotel units. Similar with the stock market, users could create and execute transaction orders for a given price and number of coins.

Technical solution

For the given business requirement, we built a custom web platform where users could invest in a fast and secured way, with a powerful user interface adapted for mobile devices and wide screens.

Feature highlights

  • multi-language platform, supporting english and romanian
  • top-up account and capability to withdraw cash
  • create, execute and cancel transaction orders
  • deposit creation capability

Time to market

We know how important is time to market for software products, so the first phase of the product went live in just two months, product being released on April 5th, 2022.
