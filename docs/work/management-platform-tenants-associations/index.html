Tenants associations management platform

Business requirements

Months ago, we got a challenge to digitalize a group of tenants associations counting more than 10.000 customers. The business requirement has been to build a product where tenants or homeowners could check their monthly receipts and pay their expenses (utilities, rents) online in a secured way.

Technical solution

With such a high number of customers, we went for a custom solution with an easy and secured on-boarding process. On the platform, customers could see data on their monthly consumption and receipts, submit meter reading data and pay due amounts online.

Feature highlights

  • secured account creation with cross-validation
  • integration with payment processor to allow online payments
  • capability to submit meter reading based on dynamic rules

Time to market

Released to customers in about one and a half months from collaboration kickoff, first phase of the platform included the capability to view monthly expenses and pay due amounts.

After a round of feedback gathering, we delivered the second phase of the platform with the ability for customers to submit their meter readings and split their due amounts in separate payments.
