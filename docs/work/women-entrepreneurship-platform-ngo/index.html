Membership & event automation platform

Business requirements

In recent years, women entrepreneurship has become a critical pillar in Romania’s economic transformation. A well-known organization dedicated to increase the importance of the role of women in the development of Romanian society sought to strengthen its digital presence and empower its growing community of women entrepreneurs. The organization needed a modern, secure, and user-friendly platform that could streamline operations, centralize data, and improve member engagement through digital transformation.

The purpose was clear ‐ create a digital ecosystem where members could connect, enroll, pay their annual membership fee, join exclusive events, and collaborate online — all within a seamless user journey.

Feature highlights

The organization previously managed memberships and events using spreadsheets, manual workflows, and email communication. This fragmented approach led to several pain points, so we designed and developed a custom membership and event management platform with modern technologies, using a modular architecture to ensure scalability and maintainability.

  • End-to-end enrollment automation where new members could register online, fill in their profile details, and complete the payment process fully digitally
  • Integrated payments through a secure integration with local payment gateway, plus automatic membership renewal reminders
  • Events management module with a dynamic events calendar, ticketing (free and paid options), automated confirmations, and attendance tracking
  • Admin dashboard with role-based access for organization administrators to manage members, track payments and create events
  • Responsive and accessible design, a mobile-first interface ensuring inclusivity and accessibility for all members

Time to market

The initial MVP was delivered in three months, allowing the organization to launch the platform ahead of its annual summit. The project demonstrates the value of digital transformation in nonprofits, showcasing how technology can drive community growth, empower members and modernize organization operations through data-drive insights and automation.
