Membership & event automation platform

Business requirements

Women entrepreneurship has become a key driver of Romania’s economic transformation. A leading organization dedicated to advancing the role of women in Romanian society wanted to strengthen its digital presence and better serve its growing community of women entrepreneurs.

They needed a modern, secure, and user-friendly platform to streamline operations, centralize member and event data, and increase engagement. The goal was to create a digital ecosystem where members can connect, enroll, pay membership fees, join exclusive events, and collaborate online in a seamless, end-to-end journey.

Technical approach

The organization previously managed memberships and events using spreadsheets, manual workflows, and email communication. This fragmented approach led to several pain points, so we designed and developed a custom membership and event management platform with modern technologies, using a modular architecture to ensure scalability and maintainability.

To further improve efficiency, the platform integrates an AI-driven microservice powered by a large language model. Through an internal API, it generates suggested summaries and SEO-optimized descriptions for events, which admins can review and approve directly in the interface. This brings generative AI into the existing workflow without adding friction.

Feature highlights

  • End-to-end enrollment automation: New members can register online, complete their profiles, and pay their annual membership fee fully digitally, reducing manual onboarding effort
  • Integrated, automated payments: Secure integration with a local payment gateway supports online payments and automatic renewal reminders, improving collection rates and member retention
  • Event management and automation: A dedicated events module with a dynamic events calendar, free and paid ticketing, automated confirmations, and attendance tracking to support ongoing community engagement
  • AI-assisted event copywriting: AI-powered suggestions turn raw event details into concise, engaging, and SEO-friendly descriptions, helping the team publish consistent, high-quality content faster
  • Admin dashboard and access control: Role-based access for organization staff to manage members, track payments, configure events, and monitor key operations from a single place

Time to market

The initial MVP was delivered in three months, allowing the organization to launch the platform in time for its annual summit. The project highlights how digital transformation can benefit nonprofits: simplifying operations, unlocking data-driven insights, and empowering members through a modern digital experience.

By centralizing membership and event management, the platform reduces manual work, increases engagement, and supports the organization’s mission of growing a strong, connected community of women entrepreneurs.
