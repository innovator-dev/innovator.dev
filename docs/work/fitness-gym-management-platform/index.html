Powering modern gyms with automated operations

Business requirements

The customer needed help structuring their operations around a single, reliable platform for managing members, memberships, and card payments, while also preparing the business for future integrations with partners such as ESX and 7Card. Their information was spread across multiple tools that did not scale well and made reporting, revenue tracking, and access control unnecessarily complex.

Technical approach

To address these challenges, we developed a custom web platform to digitalize the core business operations, focusing on efficient management of members, memberships, and card payments.

The solution was designed as a scalable digital backbone, able to support both the gym’s internal processes and future integrations with external fitness ecosystems such as 7Card and ESX.

Feature highlights

  • Member management: create, update, and organize member profiles, with a clear view of each customer’s status and active memberships
  • Flexible membership types: define and manage different memberships, including options with distinct access rules such as full-time access or limited time-slot access
  • Payment tracking: monitor transactions and payment history to provide better visibility and control over cash flow
  • Integration-ready architecture: built with interoperability in mind, making it easier to plug in external services like ESX and 7Card for access control and validation
  • Operational scalability: provides a solid foundation for future extensions such as automated access, external validations, or synchronization with other systems in the sports and fitness ecosystem

Time to market

The initial phase of the project was delivered in approximately 6–8 weeks, with additional dedicated phases for external integrations and advanced automation. Our goal was to centralize operational data, reduce manual work for the gym staff, and prepare the business for growth and partnerships in the wider fitness ecosystem.
